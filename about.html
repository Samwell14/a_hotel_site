About Hotel Samantua

LDiscover a haven of peace and luxury at Hotel Samantua. Nestled amidst lush greenery and serene landscapes, our resort offers a unique blend of comfort and natural beauty.

Key Features:
Luxurious Accommodations: Indulge in our spacious rooms and suites, each designed with your comfort in mind.
World-Class Dining: Savor culinary delights at our gourmet restaurants, offering a fusion of local and international cuisine.
Rejuvenating Spa: Pamper yourself with a range of rejuvenating treatments at our tranquil spa.
Outdoor Paradise: Explore our pristine gardens, take a dip in our infinity pool, or simply relax by the bonfire.
Adventure Awaits: Embark on thrilling outdoor activities like hiking, cycling, and birdwatching.
